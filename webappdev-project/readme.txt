Adding a Christmas theme to pages.

=== Description ===

The Christmasifier is the plugin for all your Christmasy needs! It updates your Wordpress site to match the look and feel of Christmas with just a toggle of the plugin. ‘Tis the Plugin to make your blog merrier.

Chrismasifier adds a cherry, Christmas theme to pages on a WordPress blog.  Font, background, and words are changed to fit the holiday theme.  It can be turned on or off by users on the dashboard of the site. 

=== Installation ---

1. Upload the folder to the '/wp-content/plugins/' directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the "Plugins" menu in WordPress.
3. Navigate to the Christmasifier tab in the menu and check "Activate" and "Save changes."
4. To turn off, uncheck "Activate" and "Save Changes"
